> ABOUT_PROJECT

TactiSkin is an advanced sensory textile engineered to transform how impact forces are analyzed. It brings precise, real-time data to high-stakes testing environments.

> PROBLEM_STATEMENT

> Lack of high-resolution pressure mapping in crash test systems.
> No detection of localized impact zones.
> No real-time injury visualization.

> PROPOSED_SOLUTION

Flexible textile-based smart skin using Ecoflex, conductive thread grid, Velostat sensing layer, and Arduino-based acquisition system.

Technology

  • Ecoflex 00-30 silicone layer with TPU coating for flexibility and impact distribution
  • Velostat piezoresistive sensing film (30kΩ → 5kΩ under pressure)
  • 8×8 conductive grid using silver-plated threads
  • 10kΩ voltage divider with 0.1µF capacitors for noise filtering
  • Arduino Mega (ATmega2560) for scanning and ADC conversion
  • Serial communication at 115200 baud
  • Python (Matplotlib) for real-time heatmap visualization
  • PET + Kapton insulation layers
  • 3mm neoprene base for support
