About the Product

What is TactiSkin?

TactiSkin is a next-generation flexible textile-based smart sensing system designed to revolutionize how we understand and analyze impact forces in safety-critical applications.

By weaving conductive threads through a Velostat pressure-sensitive layer encased in Ecoflex silicone, TactiSkin creates an intelligent skin that feels, maps, and visualizes every impact in real time.

  • ⚡ Textile-based smart skin using conductive threads + Velostat
  • 📡 Detects real-time pressure distribution across full surface
  • 🌡️ Converts impact data into live heatmap visualization
  • 🏎️ Designed for crash testing & SHARP helmet safety testing

Process

How It Works

Five seamless steps from physical impact to digital insight

01
👊

Pressure Applied

External force or impact is applied on the smart textile skin surface

02
🔄

Resistance Changes

Velostat layer responds — resistance drops proportionally to applied pressure

03
🔌

Arduino Reads Signals

Analog voltage signals are scanned across all 64 nodes via multiplexer circuit

04
📤

Data Transmitted

Processed readings sent over serial communication to the host software in real time

05
🌡️

Heatmap Displayed

Python renders a live, color-coded heatmap showing pressure zones across the surface
