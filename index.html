About the Product

What is 6sens?

6sens is a next-generation flexible textile-based smart sensing system designed to revolutionize how we understand and analyze impact forces in safety-critical applications.

By weaving conductive threads through a Velostat pressure-sensitive layer encased in Ecoflex silicone, 6sens creates an intelligent skin that feels, maps, and visualizes every impact in real time.

  • Textile-based smart skin using conductive threads + Velostat
  • Detects real-time pressure distribution across full surface
  • Converts impact data into live heatmap visualization
  • Designed for crash testing & SHARP helmet safety testing

Conductive Textile Matrix

64 sensing nodes woven into flexible fabric using conductive threads and Velostat piezoresistive material

SHARP Certified Testing

Purpose-built for crash test dummies and helmet impact analysis aligned with SHARP safety standards

Process

How It Works

Five seamless steps from physical impact to digital insight

01

Pressure Applied

External force or impact is applied on the smart textile skin surface

02

Resistance Changes

Velostat layer responds — resistance drops proportionally to applied pressure

03

Arduino Reads Signals

Analog voltage signals are scanned across all 64 nodes via voltage divider

04

Data Transmitted

Processed readings sent over serial communication to the host software in real time

05

Heatmap Displayed

Python renders a live, color-coded heatmap showing pressure zones across the surface
